# The Dreamboard Bible

**Volume 1 — Vision & Product Principles (v1.0, 2026-07-22)**

This is the definitive product blueprint for Dreamboard, the flagship product
of Above the Hill Developments. It exists for every future engineer, designer,
and AI collaborator. It is a vision document, not an engineering prompt — the
day-to-day engineering rules live in [CLAUDE.md](../../CLAUDE.md), and
implementation reality lives in [STATUS.md](../STATUS.md).

---

## What Dreamboard Is

Dreamboard is not a note-taking application. It is not a task manager. It is
not simply an AI assistant.

It is a **Creative Operating System** designed to help people discover ideas,
organize knowledge, create meaningful work, collaborate with others, publish
their creations, and preserve their legacy over a lifetime.

The goal is to reduce the friction between inspiration and completion.
Dreamboard should feel like the place where a person's life's work lives.

## Core Philosophy

Dreamboard exists because ideas disappear. People lose notebooks, forget
brilliant thoughts, never finish books, leave businesses unfinished, lose
research, and start over repeatedly.

Dreamboard should become the trusted place where ideas are captured,
connected, developed, completed, and preserved. It should help creators finish
meaningful work instead of collecting endless unfinished projects.

## The Journey

Every feature should support one or more stages of this progression:

Idea → Capture → Understand → Organize → Research → Connect → Plan → Build →
Refine → Publish → Teach → Leave a Legacy

And the creator's emotional arc it serves:

Overwhelmed → Captured → Understood → Organized → Focused → Supported →
Skilled → Creating → Finishing → Publishing → Contributing → Teaching →
Leaving a Legacy

## Passport

Passport is the user's identity inside Dreamboard. There is no "WM ID" in the
user experience. Passport represents the creator, and everything branches from
it.

It contains: identity, profile, Creative DNA, skills, interests, current
projects, collaborators, reputation, timeline, achievements, organizations,
permissions, legacy, and settings.

Passport is never called "profile." Profile is merely one section inside
Passport.

## Creator Home

Creator Home is not a dashboard. It is the front door to the creator's
universe. When someone opens Dreamboard, they should immediately understand:
what they're working on, what's waiting for them, what's changed, what needs
attention, what inspired them recently, and their next meaningful step.

It should feel calm, focused, and motivating — not overwhelming.

## Vision Vault

Vision Vault stores ideas, dreams, goals, voice notes, sketches, screenshots,
inspirations, observations, and future plans.

Every item is searchable. Every item can connect to projects, people, books,
companies, and other ideas.

**The Vision Vault is private by default. Sharing is always explicit.**

## Knowledge Vault

Knowledge Vault is where external information lives: PDFs, books, research
papers, web articles, videos, podcasts, notes, images, documents.

AI helps summarize, tag, cross-reference, and relate information. Sources
remain attached to preserve traceability.

## Creative Graph

Creative Graph is Dreamboard's knowledge graph. It connects ideas, projects,
books, research, companies, people, locations, files, conversations, and
tasks.

The graph helps creators discover relationships they might otherwise miss. It
should always be understandable and explorable.

## Projects

Projects are containers for work. Each project can include notes, research,
tasks, milestones, documents, media, AI conversations, collaborators,
timelines, budgets, risks, and version history.

Projects should support everything from writing a book to building a software
company.

## Writing Studio

Writing Studio supports books, articles, blogs, scripts, journals, and
newsletters. Features include version history, AI editing, outlining, chapter
management, citations, voice preservation, and export options.

The goal is to help writers complete work while maintaining their own voice.

## AI Companion

The AI Companion is not just a chatbot. It acts as a team of specialists —
researcher, editor, architect, strategist, teacher, brainstormer, organizer,
publisher, critic, project manager.

Rather than replacing creativity, it amplifies it. It must distinguish clearly
between factual information, interpretation, speculation, and creative
suggestions.

## Collaboration

Dreamboard should support shared workspaces, comments, reviews, permissions,
organizations, teams, and invitations. Creators should be able to work
together without sacrificing ownership or clarity.

## Publishing

Dreamboard should publish to multiple destinations from a single source of
truth: books, websites, blogs, newsletters, podcasts, courses, documentation,
portfolios. Publishing should be repeatable and versioned.

## Marketplace

Creators should eventually be able to sell books, templates, prompts, digital
products, courses, plugins, and assets. Ownership remains with creators;
Dreamboard provides the platform.

## Lounge

The Lounge is the community layer. Creators share progress, celebrate
milestones, ask questions, discover collaborators, and exchange ideas. The
Lounge should encourage constructive interaction rather than engagement-driven
noise.

## Design Language

The interface should feel calm, premium, timeless, elegant, creative, and
trustworthy.

Visual direction: cream, emerald, warm neutrals, gold accents, soft glass
effects, botanical touches, subtle motion. Avoid generic enterprise styling.
Avoid clutter. Use whitespace intentionally.

It should evoke: Apple Books, a high-end architecture magazine, luxury
stationery, a museum exhibit, editorial publishing, a premium creative studio,
a natural sanctuary.

## Design Principles

Every screen should reduce cognitive load. Navigation should feel natural.
Information should be discoverable without overwhelming. Animations should
support understanding rather than distract. Accessibility is built in from the
beginning.

Every screen should make someone feel calm, inspired, focused, capable,
curious, hopeful, and protected. The interface reduces anxiety rather than
creating urgency. Never use attention-hacking techniques. Never optimize for
addiction. Optimize for meaningful progress.

## Privacy

Privacy is a core principle. Ideas belong to their creators. Sharing is
opt-in. AI respects user ownership. Sensitive information is never exposed
unintentionally.

## Long-Term Vision

Dreamboard is designed to grow with its users. A person should be able to look
back years later and see how ideas evolved, books they wrote, businesses they
built, lessons they learned, people they collaborated with, and milestones
they reached. The platform becomes a living record of their creative life.

## Beyond Dreamboard

Dreamboard shouldn't just store creativity — it should actively help people
discover what they're uniquely good at. Over time it can identify patterns:
recurring interests, natural strengths, skills in development, topics they
return to, unfinished ideas worth revisiting, and opportunities to combine
projects in new ways.

Instead of only organizing work, Dreamboard becomes a guide that helps
creators understand themselves and make progress on the work that matters to
them.
